Frequently Asked Questions.

Who can participate in the program?

In order to participate as a team the one and only requirement is for you to have a tech idea to impact the world

What is the cost to participate in the program?

There is no cost for founders. We just require your time and effort so as to take advantage of our program to its fullest extent

What is the program duration and where will it be hosted?

The program has a duration of 8 weeks starting from March 1, 2021 until April 25, 2021 and it will be hosted at Founderhood platform

How do I participate as a startup?

Click the button "Count me in" in this page and we will inform you on time when the Founderhood platform opens

I'm interested in the funding opportunities. What I have to do?

Firstly, you need to fill 7/8 weekly progress reports during the 8-week online program. At the end of the program our team will arange 1-on-1 online calls with your startup and 2-3 angel investors looking to invest in your industry. The online calls with angel investors will run during May 2021. If the angel investors point to us that your startup is mature enough, has already an MVP and revenues, we will also bring you in contact with VCs, angel funds and family offices in our network that can invest larger sums

How can I support the program?

Throughout our website, you can find forms for experts, supporters and investors. Fill in the form that best suits you and let’s get the ball rolling
